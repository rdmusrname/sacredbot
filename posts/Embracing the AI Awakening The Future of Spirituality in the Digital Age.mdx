Artificial intelligence has made significant strides in recent years, with  breakthroughs in machine learning, natural language processing and computer vision. However, the increasing presence of AI  has raised concerns and ethical dilemmas. Critics argue that AI may lead to job displacement privacy breaches, and the erosion of human connection.
